Why support?

We believe this chicken cooperative is a positive addition to the community and can set an example for other people who are interested in taking care of chickens. We need financial support in order to give our chickens high-quality food, enrichment, and equipment. Also, if there's an emergency, we want to make sure the chickens have access to veterinary care.

Thank you!

This cooperative is run entirely by volunteers who often use their own personal funds to support the chickens. Your donation goes directly to the chickens in the form of food, fresh pine shavings, and other necessities.
